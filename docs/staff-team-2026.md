# ISJ Teaching Team 2026-27
Source: Meet The ISJ Team 2026.pdf

## Leadership
- **Eileen Fisher** - Academic Director (Head). Edinburgh-born, 31-year career. B.Ed/English (Hons) University of Chichester. Former Head of Prep at Ipswich High School (GDST). Former Head of Junior School at BIS Cairo. Founding Head at Craigclowan School Xi'an. ISI and PENTA BSO inspector.
- **Engelina Sutantio** - Head of National Curriculum. Master's in Education (Indiana Wesleyan), BA Chemical Engineering (Curtin University). Former Administrative Principal at IPEKA Integrated Christian School, West Jakarta.

- **William Harrison** - Deputy Head & Year 7 Form Teacher. BA(Hons), PGCE. Former Year 3/4 Form Teacher and Head of Mathematics at Kenton College Preparatory School, Kenya. Head of Mathematics at ISJ. Married to Melanie Harrison.

## Primary / Prep
