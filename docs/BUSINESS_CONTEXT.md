## Market & Competitive Position

### The Jakarta International School Market
Jakarta's international school market serves an estimated 5,000+ pupils across the premium tier alone. Indonesia is Southeast Asia's largest economy (population ~300m, GDP growth ~5%), with ASEAN on track to become the 4th-largest economy globally by 2030. Greater Jakarta has 30m+ people and is the country's financial and corporate hub. The prime catchment for international schools is the Pondok Indah to Kemang corridor in South Jakarta, where wealthy Indonesian families and the expat community are concentrated.

ISJ's target is modest relative to this market: reaching 300–400 pupils over 10 years from an addressable pool of 5,000+ represents a market share of 6–8%.

### Why Parents Choose ISJ
ISJ occupies a distinct position: British curriculum, Pondok Indah location, premium but not top-tier pricing. The competitive picture by school:

- **vs JIS (2,500 pupils):** ISJ is ~19% cheaper with British vs US curriculum. JIS is the market leader by scale but a different product.
- **vs BSJ (1,400 pupils):** ISJ is ~7% cheaper and located in Pondok Indah vs Tangerang. BSJ's Bintaro location is 60–90 minutes from the core expat catchment in typical Jakarta traffic.
- **vs ACG, NAS:** With secondary and new facilities, ISJ will clearly exceed both ACG and NAS in campus offering while maintaining the mid size-school, high-touch model parents value. ISJ is priced between these schools and the top tier.
- **vs AIS (400+ pupils):** AIS has a relatively strong campus but offers an Australian curriculum. ISJ provides the British pathway (IGCSE/A-Level) that most internationally mobile families prefer.
- **vs WCIJ (pre-launch):** Wellington College is a strong UK brand but is building in BSD City, Tangerang — ~25km from Pondok Indah, a completely different catchment area. The WCIJ campus is not large and has limited facilities. Notably, WCIJ already failed and aborted a first opening attempt across the road from ISJ in 2023. Fees are expected to be ~9% above ISJ.

With secondary, ISJ will be the clear leading British curriculum offer located in South Jakarta.
