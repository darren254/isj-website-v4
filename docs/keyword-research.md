# ISJ Keyword Research — March 2026

## Competitive Landscape Update

**Big news: Wellington College Jakarta opens August 2026.** BSD City location (not Pondok Indah), ages 3-8 initially, expanding to Year 13 within 4 years. Backed by Singaporean billionaire Peter Lim. They're positioning as "the first British-heritage school in Indonesia" — which is incorrect (BSJ has been there since 1973, ISJ since 2021), but they'll have marketing budget. ISJ needs to own the British independent school positioning before Wellington builds momentum.

**BSJ runs IB, not GCSEs/A-Levels.** This is a massive differentiator. BSJ uses IB MYP (Years 7-11) and IB DP (Years 12-13). ISJ will be the only school in Jakarta offering genuine GCSEs and A-Levels via the English National Curriculum through-school. This needs to be front and centre.

**NAS Jakarta launching Year 7 in August 2026.** Purpose-built secondary building underway. They'll go from primary-only to through-school, but on the IPC/IB pathway, not British National Curriculum. Direct competitor for families considering staying put vs moving to ISJ for secondary.

**ACG Jakarta** offers Cambridge IGCSEs and IB DP (no A-Levels). Part of the Inspired network. South Jakarta. The only other school in Jakarta offering IGCSEs, but they don't offer A-Levels — they pivot to IB DP in Sixth Form. ISJ will be the only school offering GCSEs *and* A-Levels as a pure British pathway.

**BSJ confirmed: IB not A-Levels.** BSJ uses English National Curriculum in Primary, but switches to IB MYP (Years 7-11) and IB DP (Years 12-13). Despite the "British" name, they don't offer GCSEs or A-Levels. This is ISJ's single biggest differentiator. Fees: IDR 200-350m per year depending on year group.

**Wellington College Jakarta** — opens August 2026 in BSD City. Ages 3-8 only (Nursery to Year 3). 350 students initial intake. 13,000 sqm campus. Senior School not until 2030. Backed by Peter Lim via Wellington College Education Singapore. Domain: wellingtoncollege.sch.id. Positioning as "first Wellington College in Indonesia." BSD location is 45+ minutes from Pondok Indah — different catchment area entirely.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Own "GCSE" and "A-Levels" + "Jakarta" immediately.** No one else can claim this. Build the senior school pages with strong SEO even before the campus opens.
2. **Wellington College is the new threat.** They open August 2026 in BSD. Different location but same "British premium" positioning. ISJ must be established online before Wellington's marketing ramps up.
3. **BSJ's weakness is ISJ's strength.** BSJ doesn't offer GCSEs/A-Levels — they're IB. For families who specifically want the British qualification pathway, ISJ is the only option in Jakarta.
4. **Bahasa keywords matter.** Indonesian parents search in Bahasa. The existing language pages are a great start but need proper i18n with full content, not just translated landing pages.
5. **Comparison and tools content wins.** ISJ already has the school comparison tool and commute times tool. These need to be first-class features on the new site, not buried in the footer.
6. **Expat guide content is already strong.** ISJ's Insights blog is already appearing in search results for expat-related queries. Keep this momentum.
