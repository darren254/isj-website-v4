Usage Notes

  • Use the navy logo on light backgrounds (cream, white).
  • Use the white logo on dark backgrounds (navy, photography).
  • Use the gold logo sparingly for accent or special applications.
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the crown.
  • Never stretch, rotate, recolour, or add effects to the logo.
  • For web use, the 400px and 800px exports are sufficient. Use 1600px for print and large-format display.
