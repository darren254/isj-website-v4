Admissions

Come and See for Yourself

The best way to know whether ISJ is right for your family is to visit. Walk the corridors, look in on a lesson, meet the teachers who will know your child by name. Most families say the visit is what made their decision.

Tours are personalised. Each visit covers the parts of the school that matter most to your family, at the pace your family needs.

What to Expect

A Morning at ISJ

Your tour is guided by a senior member of staff who knows every child and every classroom. You will see lessons in progress, meet teachers, and get a real sense of the school day.

Tours typically last around an hour, scheduled during the school day so you see ISJ as it really is.

Bringing your child along is encouraged. For families relocating to Jakarta, ISJ also offers live video tours with the admissions team.

"A lovely campus with great teachers… it makes the place more joyful."

Before You Visit

Practical Details

When to Visit

Tours run on school days during term time, typically between 8:30am and 11:00am, when lessons are in full swing so you see the school at its best.

Where to Find Us

ISJ is in Pondok Indah, South Jakarta. When you arrive, reception will welcome you and your guide will meet you there. Visitor parking is available on site.

Video Tours

For families relocating to Jakarta, ISJ offers a live video tour with the admissions team. The same personal experience, wherever you are.

After the Tour

If you decide ISJ is right for your family, the admissions team will walk you through the next steps. You can also reach the team directly on the contact page.
