Entry Points

The main entry points are Pre-Nursery (entering Pre-Prep), Reception, Year 7 (entering Senior School), and Year 12. Places may be available in any year group, and mid-year transfers are welcome subject to available space.

Assessment

Pre-Nursery, Nursery, and Reception have no formal assessment. From Year 1, children in Prep School complete an age-appropriate assessment in literacy, numeracy, and reasoning.
